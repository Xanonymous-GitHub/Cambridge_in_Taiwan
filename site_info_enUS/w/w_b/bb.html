Project Structure

      Two decades ago, an old professor of Chung Hsing University made a suggestion to the Third Rivers Bureau of the Water Resources Department, hoping that they could transform the area located on the border between the old county and city into a Water environment suitable for all ages. To this end, the river bureau found many experts to assess how to build a future as a row of governance and ecological environment protection demonstration area, but the regional drainage has long been the main task of flood discharge.

      The impact on the ecological environment is difficult to take into account. Therefore, unlike the general flood control measures with wave block, it is based on green and low-carbon strategies: reducing artificial structures, using natural materials as much as possible, and building and improving them according to the characteristics of streams.
