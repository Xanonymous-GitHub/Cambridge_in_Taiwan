Local people

       On the north-west side of Da-Li river live about 12,000 people. In order to maintain the environment of Cambridge, they composed a volunteer team to protect the environment.

       Cambridge attracted many tourists after its construction. However, it also makes Cambridge dirtier than before. Due to the result, 77 residents composed a volunteer team to clean and maintain the area. Fortunately, they got 2nd place on the National Water Resources Conservation Competition. I's a big honor.
