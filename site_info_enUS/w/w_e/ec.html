The Chief of Yong Lung Sub-district ,Shi-Yuan Lin

In the Cambridge project, Lin Shi yuan is the bridge between the government and the people. The remediation process will bring a lot of inconveniences and noises, so Chief Lin not only had to listen to the needs of the people, but also to communicate with the city hall. Chief Lin played an important role in environmental protection. He assisted to organize local people to maintain the cleanness of the waterfront.

Cambridge Water Quality and Environment

Image source: photographed by ourselves

      We asked Chief Lin a question, "Why can the water quality in Cambridge still not get improved?" Chief Lin answered, " With regard to the sewage treatment section, at present, domestic wastewater is discharged directly into the river while industrial wastewater is treated first. "

       Chief Lin added, “the Cambridge Rezoning Plan will be expanded to Big Cambridge Project. That is, the city hall and the central government have passed a budget to treat domestic wastewater on a bigger scale. Yet, it will not be completed until next year. This project also aims to make Taichung become a base to promote canoeing sport and strive to make the canoeing as an official competition in 2019 East Asian Youth Games.”

Cambridge conservation volunteer team

Image source: photographed by ourselves

      Talking about the volunteer team organization, Chief Lin said: "The volunteer members are 60 in total. There are one captain, two vice-captains and one financial officer. They are divided into four teams of 15 people and one team captain. Although they are local volunteer teams, they systematically gear up to help make Cambridge a better place.”
