Hui-Wen canoe team

      Our canoe school team won lots of prizes because of coach Lee, including 4 silver medals in Incheon Asian games 2014, 2 gold medals, 2 silver medals and 3 bronze medals in Taichung Mayor Cup indoor canoe races2012, second place in National Secondary School Sports canoe water polo and racing championship 2012, and 3 gold medals, 2 silver medals, 2 bronze medals in the National Secondary School Sports Championship.

      On top of it, in the upcoming Asian canoe game 2018, 7 out of 8 national representative players are from Hui-Wen High school, all of whom used to be instructed by coach Lee.

       Behind those shining rewards was a canoe team that had no professional training place but practiced in a little swimming pool. So Wan-Ru Lin, the president of Canoe Association and coach Lee strove to make Cambridge a formal training place for canoeing.
