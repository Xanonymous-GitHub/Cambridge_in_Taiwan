Motivation

      As the students of Hui-Wen high school，we are lucky to participate the canoeing activities assisted by our school canoeing team. After the activities, we are surprised to learn that the coach and a group of volunteers have made tremendous contribution to the restoration of the rivers of our city.

      Every canoeing activity in school was organized and instructed by couch Lee. Thus, when we heard the news of his passing away, we decided to make a web page for couch Lee and all the touching stories behind Cambridge in Taichung Taiwan.
