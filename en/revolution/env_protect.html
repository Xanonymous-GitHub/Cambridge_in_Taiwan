Environmental protection

      Nowadays, water area decreased by 38 square kilometers due to the growing population of the city. Therefore, every time the heavy rain came, floods would happen. The drainage section on midstream has been occupied by citizens and rivers are jammed with the waste from factories. These reasons caused the drainage environment crashed.

       Both banks have been highly developed and it makes the restoration of the river harder and harder.
