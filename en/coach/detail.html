Devoting All His Life to Canoeing

      Our teacher, Rong-Wen Lee , the Pioneer of Canoeing in Taiwan, passed away on September 4. Most national canoeing players were once his students. He is also the first person who introduced to Taiwan the canoeing skills and coaches from France. Without him , canoeing could not prosper in our country as it does now. Most importantly, because he used to dedicate all his time to finding an ideal place for the players to practice, consequently establishing the practicing area in Sun Moon lake and Cambridge in Taichung.

       Once a best canoeing player, a best canoeing couch, and a best citizen, coach Lee sacrificed his money as well as his life to make canoeing a popular sport in our country. Moreover, it is he that made the Cambridge in Taiwan come true and made the rivers around it much cleaner than before.
