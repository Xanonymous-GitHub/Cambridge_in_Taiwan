Field study

2017/8/21    Yo-Yu Lin, the assistant of Yao-Zun Chang city councilor

      It was the first time we went out for the interview. At first, we were so nervous that we could not even speak fluently. But after the guide by director and the nice talk with the assistant, we finally got relaxed. After the interview, we understood the association and the procedure of passing a budget case. These are all key points to make the construction of Cambridge come true.

2017/8/21    Shi-Yan Lin, the chief of Yun-Lon Li, Da-Li district, Taichung

      The chief told us that maintaining Cambridge is every citizen's duty. Local people formed a volunteer team in order to keep a clean environment for Cambridge. The chief also mention that the construction of Cambridge is one of the most successful community developments in Taiwan.

2017/8/24     Third Rivers Bureau of the Water Resources Department of the Ministry of Economic Affairs -Chief Jay-Yung Zen

      During the interview, although we could not understand some professional terms, after the Chief explained patiently to the follow-up information provided by the River Bureau, we could understand why the project was originally a flood control project. Since the Ministry of Interior and Taichung City cooperated with the local residents , they finally broke through all kinds of restrictions, making Cambridge Waterfront Park a model for river restoration.

2017/8/26     2017 NTSO Tuen District International Arts Festival - Mozart odd event

      8 / 24-26 A three-day event. On the 26th, our team came to Cambridge Waterfront Park to watch the show with local residents. The stage set up by the other side allowed us to see the performers more clearly and the lights shone through the slightly flowing river. We were all overwhelmed b by the high-level performance and really appreciated all the efforts made by the residents and volunteers.

2017/8/27    Wan-Ru Lin, the Chairwoman of Canoe Association

      Through this interview with the board chairman, we found that behind the pleasant environment of Cambridge, there is a romantic story.

       This day was also the "National Water Sports Carnival" co-organized by the Canoe Association and the Taichung Southern Sports Association. Although we did not personally experience the design of the barges, when we interviewed the experienced people, they said," We Like this event very much. This is the first time everyone can experience the new strange and fun sport. Also, we were stunned by the organized and enchanting environment."

2017/9/22    Hui Wen high School Canoe Team Members

      Four canoe players were interviewed by us. They all personally got involved in the activities of Cambridge. In the beginning, they also needed to clean out the weeds and rubbish in the river course.

      When they learned the news of the death of Coach Lee, they all felt deeply grieved. “Coach Lee is very strict in training. However, without Coach Li, we could never have such an ideal place to practice or get successes in the canoeing."
